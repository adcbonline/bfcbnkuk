Accessibility for customers and partners

BFC Bank - Accessibility statement

It is very important to us at BFC Bank that our web site and online banking is accessible for all customers and partners and we are always working to improve this. We have tried to make our website as accessible as possible by working to the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C)'s Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG).

For further help if you have problems seeing the screen, find it difficult to use the mouse or keyboard or need help with language or reading please visit AbilityNet's Useful links web site For easy access to instructions and advice for specific settings and adjustments for your computer, tablet or smartphone the links below demonstrate how to improve the readability and accessibility of our web site and online portal.

AbilityNet is a national charity helping disabled adults and children use computers and the internet by adapting and adjusting their technology. It also provides a free service to individuals with disabilities to assess their computer needs you can call them free on 0800 269 545 (UK telephone number) for more information or email enquiries@abilitynet.org.uk
